What is Secure the Bag?

Secure The Bag keeps you in control of your finances by sending timely SMS alerts straight to your phone. No more logging into multiple banking apps—our platform does the heavy lifting for you. Simply choose how many alerts you want per day (1, 2, or 3), connect your bank accounts through our secure integration, and get real-time updates on your balance and recent transactions. It’s never been easier to stay on top of your money.

Solution

Step by step

1. Sign Up

Create an account and pick the plan that fits your lifestyle—whether you need one text a day or multiple updates. We’ll guide you through a quick setup process to ensure you’re ready to start receiving alerts.

2. Connect Your Bank

Link your bank accounts using our secure Plaid integration. This lets us access real-time balance and transaction data without storing any sensitive login details. Your info stays safe and encrypted at all times.

3. Customize Your Alerts

Decide how many alerts you want and when you want them—morning, evening, or any time in between. You can also choose the level of detail you’d like, from just your current balance to the last few transactions.

4. Sit Back & Secure The Bag

Once everything’s set, we’ll send you SMS updates on your schedule. You’ll know exactly when your balance changes, helping you dodge overdraft fees and unexpected charges.

Benefits

  • No more juggling multiple banking apps or worrying about hidden fees. Our SMS alerts cut through the noise, so you can focus on what matters—building your future.

  • With real-time balance checks, you’ll see potential red flags before they become a problem. Think of it as a financial safety net that has your back.

  • Choose how often you get updates, and adjust your preferences anytime. Whether you’re hustling, studying, or working on your next big idea, you decide how and when you want to stay informed.

  • We use trusted banking integrations and best-in-class encryption to keep your data safe. You control which accounts are linked, and you can update or remove them at any time. permission model for data + AI

  • With Secure The Bag, you’ll always know exactly where your money stands, so you can make smarter moves, faster.

Frequently Asked Questions

Have a question?

Q: How does billing work?

A: You’re billed monthly at the rate you select—$7, $10, or $13/month—depending on how many SMS alerts you want each day. Your subscription renews automatically, but you can cancel anytime with no long-term commitment.

Q: What happens if I cancel my subscription?

A: Cancellation is simple and hassle-free. Once you cancel, your subscription will not renew at the end of your current billing cycle. You can manage or cancel your subscription anytime through your account settings.

Q: Are there any hidden fees?

A: No. The price you see is the price you pay. Our subscription model is all-inclusive with no hidden fees or extra charges.

Q: How do I connect my bank account?

A: Simply log into your account, navigate to the bank connection section, and follow the prompts. Our Plaid integration ensures your account details are linked securely and quickly.

Q: How do I update my SMS alert preferences?

A: You can customize your alert schedule—whether that’s one, two, or three texts per day—directly from your profile settings. Adjust your preferences anytime to fit your routine.

Security & Data Privacy

But what about my data?
